Media Tag

Media tag means to integrate video,audio,youtube video with website.

  • You have to simply use audio tag with src inside it to integrate audio in your website.You also have to write controls to get access of audio.
  • You have to simply use video tag with src inside it to integrate video in your website.You also have to write controls to get access of video.You can also write autoplay to play video itself after opening website.
  • For embedding Youtube video you have to use iframe.You must have to use embed/.You can also write allowfullscreen to full screen your youtube video.


Multi-Page Websites

Multi page websites means where multiple html pages exist.

It can be done with the help of anchor tag.
